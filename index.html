Promoting Cyber-Self Defense Through Secure Password Practice

"Using an easy-to-guess password is like locking the door but leaving the key in the lock.
Weak passwords can quickly be broken by computer hackers."
- CISA

A strong password must contain:

  • 14 or more characters in total
  • Both uppercase and lowercase characters
  • At least one numeric character
  • At least one symbol
